Who is Charlie Kirk?
Answer: Charlie Kirk was the 31-year-old co-founder of Turning Point USA and the host of the top-rated podcast and nationally syndicated Salem radio program, “The Charlie Kirk Show.” He was the author of four books and a leading social media personality, best known for his on-campus rallies and conservative activism.
